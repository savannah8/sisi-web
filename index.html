WHO ARE WE?

SISI Pharmaceuticals Ltd. is an importer, distributor and marketer of pharmaceutical and skincare products. The company was founded in 2006 and we aim to provide market leadership for innovative pharmaceutical,diagnostic and health-care through our core pillars:
1. Regulatory support
2. Sales And Marketing
3. Distribution




OUR DNA.
  1. SISI pharmaceuticals is an indigenous African Institution which leverages on local content in East and West Africa to drive its commercial plans.

  2. SISI pharmaceuticals continues to invest in the advancement and development of the African pharmaceutical industry.

  3. SISI pharmaceuticals sees the regulator as an important partner in the business.

  4. A people-driven focus:patients,customers,partners and staff members are at the heart of our business.



OUR VISION.

To be a leading importer,distributor and marketer of pharmaceutical and health-care products.



OUR MISSION.

To progressively tailor our product offering service suite and engagement practice to meet the dynamic needs of the people we serve and promote access to superior healthcare solutions by:

  1. Continuously seeking to improve the quality of health care of patients in Africa.

  2. Constantly seeking new and innovative ways to improve the effectiveness of our value-adding role as the vital interface between the manufacturer and consumer.

  3. Creating enabling local and international partnerships,and supporting networks that work to promote professionalism and best practices within the industry.
